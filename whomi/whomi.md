### **🖼️ Formosa Vision: The Open-Source Taiwanese Image Dataset**

*2025年9月 \- 2025年11月*

While most community efforts focus on text-based data, this project addresses the scarcity of Traditional Chinese visual datasets.

* **Objective:** Establish the "Formosa Vision Dialogue Corpus" using images from the **National Cultural Memory Bank 2.0** (OGDL license) to enhance VLM's understanding of Taiwan's landmarks, history, and culture.  
* **Execution:** Led a community team (2-5 mentees) to construct visual dialogues using VLMs, with human review ensuring cultural fidelity. Organized weekly meetings for quality control and localization strategy.  
* **Impact:** An experiment in transforming cultural memory into AI-readable language, fostering collective creation for localized AI development.  
* **相關連結：**  
  * [Hugging Face Dataset](https://huggingface.co/datasets/twinkle-ai/Formosa-Vision)  
  * [iThome 報導](https://www.ithome.com.tw/pr/172192)
